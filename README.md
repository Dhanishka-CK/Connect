## What is Connect?

Connect is a web-based platform built to support children with special needs — particularly those on the autism spectrum — by closing the communication gap between the three people who matter most in their development: **parents**, **teachers**, and **therapists**.

Right now, a child's day is split across very different environments. What happens in a therapy session rarely makes it back to the classroom. What a teacher notices on Monday might not reach the parent until Friday. These gaps aren't anyone's fault — they're a structural problem. Connect is an attempt to fix that.

The idea is simple: give everyone involved in a child's care one shared space to set goals, track progress, and stay aligned. Less fragmentation, more consistency, better outcomes.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Children with special needs thrive on consistency. But the adults supporting them — parents, teachers, therapists — often work in silos, using different systems, speaking in different contexts, and rarely getting the full picture at the same time.

This leads to:
- Learning strategies that contradict each other across settings
- Developmental milestones that slip through the cracks
- Parents who feel out of the loop between appointments
- Therapists and teachers who can't build on each other's work

Connect brings everyone into the same room — digitally — so the child at the center always has a coordinated support system around them.

---

## Who It's Built For

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Parents
You know your child better than anyone. Connect gives you a window into what's happening at school and in therapy, so you can reinforce those same strategies at home. Track milestones, participate in goal-setting, and stay genuinely informed — not just updated after the fact.

### 👩‍🏫 Teachers
Managing individualized plans for multiple students is hard. Connect gives teachers a structured way to set academic and behavioral goals, log progress, and share observations with the rest of the care team — without adding another layer of administrative work.

### 🧠 Therapists
Therapy doesn't stop when the session ends. Connect lets therapists recommend exercises that can continue at home and in the classroom, monitor how interventions are working over time, and collaborate with teachers and parents to keep approaches consistent across environments.

---

## Core Features

### 📊 Dashboard
A central view of everything that matters — current goals, recent progress, pending activities, and quick updates from all three sides of the care team. No hunting through emails or waiting for the next appointment.

### 🎯 Goal Tracking
Set short-term and long-term goals with input from everyone involved. Track progress through structured updates, and keep all stakeholders accountable with clear visibility into what's been done and what's next.

### 🤝 Shared Collaboration Space
Every role sees the same child. Different perspectives, shared visibility. Teachers, parents, and therapists can each contribute their piece of the picture — and actually see how it fits together.

## Current Status

This project is currently in its **design and frontend development phase**. The core user experience and interface structure are taking shape, but there's a lot of road still ahead.

What's planned next:
- Real-time progress tracking with data persistence
- AI-powered activity recommendations tailored to individual children
- Smarter insights to help caregivers spot patterns and respond early
- Deeper integrations to reduce manual data entry across the team

---

## Why This Matters

There's a lot of edtech out there. Most of it is built for neurotypical learners in standard classroom settings. Connect is specifically for the kids who need more — more coordination, more consistency, more care — and for the adults who are already giving everything they have to provide it.

The goal isn't to replace the human connection between a child and their caregivers. It's to make that connection more informed, more consistent, and a little less exhausting to maintain.
