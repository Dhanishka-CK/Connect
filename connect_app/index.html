🎯 About

  • Connect is a personalized web-based platform designed to support the holistic development of specially-abled children by bridging the communication gap between parents, teachers, and therapists. It enables collaborative goal setting, progress tracking, resource sharing, and activity monitoring in a simple, accessible, and user-friendly interface.

🧠 Our Support Network

Together, we build brighter futures with:

  • 🧑‍🏫 Class Teachers – nurturing and supporting growth
  • 🗣️ Speech Therapists – improving communication skills
  • 👐 Occupational Therapists – enabling daily activities
  • 🧍 Physiotherapists – enhancing mobility and strength
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Parents & Guardians – always connected
